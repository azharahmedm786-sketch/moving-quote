Refund & Cancellation Policy

At PackZen, we understand that plans change. This policy explains your rights when you cancel a booking and how refunds are processed.

Quick summary: Cancel 24+ hours before your move for a full refund. Cancel within 24 hours or after driver assignment for a partial refund. No refund once packing has started.

Cancellation Timeline & Refund Amounts

How to Cancel

You can cancel your booking in two ways:

  • Via your dashboard: Login → My Bookings → select your booking → tap "✕ Cancel". This is the fastest method.
  • Via WhatsApp or call: Contact us at 9945095453 or WhatsApp with your booking reference number.

Refund Method

Refunds are always processed to the original payment method used during booking:

  • Razorpay payments (UPI, credit/debit card, net banking) — refunded to the same account/card
  • "Pay at Drop" bookings — no advance was collected, so no refund is applicable
  • Partial advance payments — only the advance amount is eligible for refund; remaining balance due at delivery is cancelled
⚠️ Please note: Razorpay payment gateway fees (typically 2%) are non-refundable as they are charged at the time of transaction by Razorpay. Your refund will be the eligible amount minus gateway fees.

Rescheduling (Free)

You can reschedule your move for free up to 12 hours before the scheduled time, subject to driver availability. Rescheduling is not considered a cancellation and no fees apply. Use the " Reschedule" button in your dashboard or contact us.

PackZen-Initiated Cancellations

In rare cases, PackZen may need to cancel your booking due to driver unavailability, severe weather, or other operational issues. In such cases:

  • You will be notified at least 4 hours in advance (where possible)
  • A 100% refund will be processed within 3–5 business days
  • We will prioritize rescheduling you at no extra cost

Damage & Goods Claims

If any of your goods are damaged during the move, you must:

  • Report the damage within 24 hours of delivery by contacting us on WhatsApp or calling us
  • Provide photos of the damaged item(s) and the original booking reference
  • Our team will assess the claim within 3 business days and respond with a resolution

PackZen's liability for damaged goods is limited to the market replacement value of the item, up to a maximum of ₹10,000 per booking, unless additional insurance was purchased. We recommend high-value items (antiques, artwork, electronics above ₹50,000) be transported in your personal vehicle.

⚠️ PackZen is not liable for damage to goods that were not professionally packed by our team, or that were packed by the customer against our team's advice.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

At PackZen, we understand that plans change. This policy explains your rights when you cancel a booking and how refunds are processed.

💡 Quick summary: Cancel 24+ hours before your move for a full refund. Cancel within 24 hours or after driver assignment for a partial refund. No refund once packing has started.

Cancellation Timeline & Refund Amounts

When you cancel Refund on advance paid Processing time
More than 48 hours before move 100% Full Refund 5–7 business days
24–48 hours before move 100% Full Refund 5–7 business days
12–24 hours before move 75% Refund 5–7 business days
Less than 12 hours before move 50% Refund 7–10 business days
After driver has been assigned 50% Refund + ₹300 cancellation fee 7–10 business days
After packing has started No Refund —
Full payment (5% discount applied) Refund as per timeline above, excluding 5% discount 7–10 business days

How to Cancel

You can cancel your booking in two ways:

  • Via your dashboard: Login → My Bookings → select your booking → tap "✕ Cancel". This is the fastest method.
  • Via WhatsApp or call: Contact us at 9945095453 or WhatsApp with your booking reference number.

Refund Method

Refunds are always processed to the original payment method used during booking:

  • Razorpay payments (UPI, credit/debit card, net banking) — refunded to the same account/card
  • "Pay at Drop" bookings — no advance was collected, so no refund is applicable
  • Partial advance payments — only the advance amount is eligible for refund; remaining balance due at delivery is cancelled
⚠️ Please note: Razorpay payment gateway fees (typically 2%) are non-refundable as they are charged at the time of transaction by Razorpay. Your refund will be the eligible amount minus gateway fees.

Rescheduling (Free)

You can reschedule your move for free up to 12 hours before the scheduled time, subject to driver availability. Rescheduling is not considered a cancellation and no fees apply. Use the "📅 Reschedule" button in your dashboard or contact us.

PackZen-Initiated Cancellations

In rare cases, PackZen may need to cancel your booking due to driver unavailability, severe weather, or other operational issues. In such cases:

  • You will be notified at least 4 hours in advance (where possible)
  • A 100% refund will be processed within 3–5 business days
  • We will prioritize rescheduling you at no extra cost

Damage & Goods Claims

If any of your goods are damaged during the move, you must:

  • Report the damage within 24 hours of delivery by contacting us on WhatsApp or calling us
  • Provide photos of the damaged item(s) and the original booking reference
  • Our team will assess the claim within 3 business days and respond with a resolution

PackZen's liability for damaged goods is limited to the market replacement value of the item, up to a maximum of ₹10,000 per booking, unless additional insurance was purchased. We recommend high-value items (antiques, artwork, electronics above ₹50,000) be transported in your personal vehicle.

⚠️ PackZen is not liable for damage to goods that were not professionally packed by our team, or that were packed by the customer against our team's advice.

Privacy Policy

PackZen respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it. We comply with India's Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Password (stored encrypted — we never see it in plain text)

When you make a booking:

  • Pickup and drop addresses
  • Moving date, house type, items to be moved
  • Photos of items you upload
  • Payment information (processed by Razorpay — we do not store card details)

Automatically collected:

  • Device type, browser, IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Location (only when you click "Use my current location" — not tracked in background)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To confirm and fulfil your booking
  • To assign a driver and coordinate your move
  • To send booking confirmations, status updates via WhatsApp/SMS/email
  • To process payments and issue GST invoices
  • To improve our services and resolve disputes
  • To send promotional offers (only if you opt in)
✅ We never sell your data to third parties. We never share your data with advertisers.

3. Data Sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Assigned driver: Your name and phone number (so they can contact you)
  • Razorpay: Payment processing (their privacy policy applies)
  • Firebase (Google): Database, authentication, and hosting
  • Google Maps: Address autocomplete and distance calculation
  • MSG91 / Twilio: SMS notifications (if enabled)

All third-party services we use are GDPR-compliant and follow industry-standard security practices.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Booking records: Retained for 7 years (required for GST compliance)
  • Chat messages: Retained for 6 months after delivery
  • Photos uploaded: Retained for 3 months after delivery

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you — email us at support@packzen.in
  • Correct inaccurate data — update via your profile in the app
  • Delete your account and data — email us with subject "Delete My Data"
  • Opt out of marketing — uncheck email/SMS preferences in your profile
  • Data portability — request a copy of your data in a readable format

We will respond to all data requests within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for authentication (keeping you logged in) and theme preferences (dark/light mode). We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser, but this will prevent you from staying logged in.

7. Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
  • Passwords are hashed using Firebase Auth (bcrypt)
  • Database access is restricted via Firestore security rules
  • Payment data never touches our servers — processed entirely by Razorpay

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it immediately.
